I am a Researcher at Microsoft Research, where I focus on structuring medical text for precision health. I obtained my Ph.D. at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Benjamin Van Durme and Kevin Duh. Prior to JHU, I was a master student at Peking University with Yansong Feng.
